Welcome To The Future of Portable Gaming!

Nintendo proudly presents the Game Boy-our brand new revolutionary handheld gaming system that allows you to take your favorite games anywhere! Featuring a lightweight design, an easy to see dot matrix display and long lasting battery that you can enjoy anywhere, the Game Boy brings a brand new experiance to our lineup.

Whether you are traveling, relaxing on your couch or spending time with your friends the Game Boy makes the gaming experience more accessible and portable than ever before. This is a new era of gaming we are excited for you to get your hands on.
