A History of Nintendo

Founded in 1889, Nintendo has a long standing history of creating entertainment products that bring joy to people of all ages. From our humble beginings as a card game manufacturer to now with electronic entertainment. We at Nintendo contiune to explore new ways to create joyful and memorable experiances.

With the launch of our Nintendo Game Boy our company is proudly leading a new chapter in portable entertainment. Designed with quality, creativity and accessibility in mind, the Game Boy reflects our commitment to exellence.

Our Mission

Our mission here at Nintendo is to create unique entertainment experiences that brings smiles to people around the world. We believe that great games should be simple to play, enjoyable for everyone and built to last a lifetime.
