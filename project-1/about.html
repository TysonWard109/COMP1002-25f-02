A New Era of Portable Entertainment

The Nintendo Game Boy is the result of years of research, development and innovation by our hard working engineers and designers here at Nintendo. Built to deliver our signature high quality entertainment in a portable format. The Game Boy will change how and where you can enjoy video games.

Innovative Features

At the heart of our Game Boy is a crisp and clean dot matrix display that is easy to read in all sorts of lighting conditions. Paired with a strong directional pad and responsive action buttons, the Game Boy offers precise control and a comfortable gaming experience for all ages.

The Game Boy is powered by replaceable batteries, giving players the freedom to enjoy hours of gameplay without having to be plugged in. It's small size and lightweight design makes it perfect to take anywhere! Whether you are staing at home, on a long car ride or spending time outdoors!

Our Expanding Game Library

Nintendo is committed to delivering a growing catalog of Game Boy titles. From puzzle games to adventure games with action packed challenges, our line up of games offer something for everyone. Each of our games are designed to take full advantage of the Game Boy's capabilities while staying true to our core vision of quality and fun.

Built By Nintendo, Designed for Everyone

With it's simply controls, sturdy construction and our family friendly approach, the Game Boy embodies our philosophy, which is that entertainment should be accessible, enjoyable and available to everyone. We at Nintendo are proud to introduce a system that opens the world of handheld gaming like never before
