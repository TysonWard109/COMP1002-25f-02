We Are Here to Help

Nintendo is commited to providing reliable support for your Game Boy system. If you need any assistance with things such as setup, gameplay or troubleshooting, the information below will get you back to playing as quickly as possible.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • The Game Boy will not turn on: Ensure that four AA batteries are installed correctly and have a charge
  • The screen is hard to see: Use the contrast adjustment dial located on the side of the system
  • Games are not loading properly: Remove the game cartridge and reinsert it firmly into the Game Boy

Customer Assistance
